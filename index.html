The Benefits:


When you navigate to another game, your previous game does not close out. Because of this, you can have multiple games running at once. You can also request new games using the 'Request a Game' button. I check this form regularly, so the game will most likely be added. However, some games are inaccessible, and if so, I will send an email describing why the game cannot be added.
